# Strategy Playbook

Recommendation definitions for restaurant-level strategic actioning.

## Scale Aggressively

- Restaurants: 175 (10.3%)
- Business meaning: Push expansion through new zones, capacity upgrades, and growth investments while preserving current unit economics.
- Apply when: Use when GPI is high and both cost discipline and revenue quality are strong with manageable channel risk.

Example assignment reasons:
- High Potential (GPI 76.6) with no major risk flags supports growth acceleration.
- High Potential (GPI 71.0) with strong cost discipline and revenue quality supports accelerated expansion.

## Expand Carefully

- Restaurants: 69 (4.1%)
- Business meaning: Pursue growth in staged pilots with strict guardrails on costs, conversion, and payback windows.
- Apply when: Use when growth potential is high or improving but risk indicators (cost pressure, channel dependence, or execution strain) are present.

Example assignment reasons:
- Moderate Potential (GPI 57.1) with meaningful headroom suggests cautious, milestone-based expansion.
- Moderate Potential (GPI 59.0) with meaningful headroom suggests cautious, milestone-based expansion.

## Rebalance Channels

- Restaurants: 353 (20.8%)
- Business meaning: Shift mix toward healthier channels by reducing aggregator over-dependence and strengthening direct/self-delivery economics.
- Apply when: Use when aggregator dependence and delivery mix are high relative to profitability quality or in-store resilience.

Example assignment reasons:
- Moderate Potential (GPI 51.6) and elevated aggregator dependence indicate channel rebalancing is the highest-leverage action.
- Moderate Potential (GPI 48.4) and elevated aggregator dependence indicate channel rebalancing is the highest-leverage action.

## Optimize

- Restaurants: 575 (33.9%)
- Business meaning: Improve efficiency and conversion in the current footprint before major expansion moves.
- Apply when: Use when performance is moderate and the business needs KPI tuning rather than structural turnaround.

Example assignment reasons:
- Moderate Potential (GPI 65.0) is best improved through operational and commercial optimization.
- Moderate Potential (GPI 56.9) is best improved through operational and commercial optimization.

## Stabilize Operations

- Restaurants: 524 (30.9%)
- Business meaning: Prioritize operational reset: margin recovery, cost control, and service consistency before pursuing growth.
- Apply when: Use when GPI is low and core economics show clear stress.

Example assignment reasons:
- Caution Zone (GPI 30.6) with weak economic quality indicates stabilization should precede growth actions.
- Caution Zone (GPI 38.6) with weak economic quality indicates stabilization should precede growth actions.
